NSS BITS PILANI
Not me but you

NSS BITS Pilani is composed of enthusiastic volunteers, under the guidance of respected teachers, who always keep in mind comprehensive and sustainable models for development. In addition to the main goals of community development, NSS also aims to inculcate service values ​​in students, and ultimately make them serious and responsible citizens dedicated to improving the country and the world.

About Us

What are we

NSS Bits Pilani has been in existence for more than 50 years and consists of more than 200 active volunteers guided by esteemed professors who have always kept in mind comprehensive and sustainable models for development.

What we do

Spreading happiness through community service has been one of the primary objectives of NSS. With innovative activities across the departments catering to all classes of the campus residents, we try to create small, happy and memorable moments in their lives. We aim to bring about a positive change in the society with focus on education and development.

Our Departments

CLP

Computer Literacy Programme is an initiative to provide basic computer literacy to the people of Pilani and nearby villages who do not have access to any computer education. Our vision is a bright and digitally successful India. The Computer Literacy Programme has equipped the learners with basic computer knowledge, coding skills, and digital awareness.

D3

DeSCo aims to bridge the gap between industry and rural development through the vehicle of Corporate Social Responsibility. It raises funds for events conducted by our organization like Conferencia De Youth, Junoon, Blood Donation Camp and Shop For A Smile.

Events

Team Events, NSS BITS Pilani has always strived hard to extend its hand in the fields of social welfare while bringing the NSS community together. With a plethora of events in an offline semester like Blood Donation Camp, Conferencia de Youth, Sports Day, and Udaan which are organised in the even semester, we did not allow online proceedings to deter us from this. With the world going online, we took some of our events online while including new feathers to the cap.

HPA

The department of Health and Public Awareness aims to spread awareness about important health and social issues and helps society in fighting against all odds. We work to empower those in need so that they can live a more comfortable life and make well-informed decisions. With our initiatives, we have managed to cover several health, employment, literacy, and sanitation issues in and around Pilani.

Parishodh

At NSS BITS Pilani we believe that engineering meets social service. Volunteers at Parishodh strive to find cost-efficient but effective solutions to several grassroot level issues faced by communities in and around Pilani. Our vision is to drive a socio-economic change towards sustainable development using Science and Technology as an enabling tool.

School

School is NSS' significant endeavour to provide quality education to the students of Pilani and the neighbouring areas. It seeks to empower students to encompass academic, logical, linguistic abilities along with imparting moral principles, social and health awareness to assure individual's overall development.

Umang

Project Umang aims to provide quality, low-cost education to all the meritorious but needy students living in and around Pilani. Our mission is to maximize education’s reach to students from these indignant families and award them the key to a bright and rewarding future to unlock their golden door of freedom from illiteracy.
